  • A little About Me

    Jacob Chong is a Illustrator, Motion Graphics, and Interactive Designer. He first got schooling at Langara's Design Formation Program, getting a Diploma. Now he is in his third year at Capilano University's IDEA school of design program in hopes to learn even more.

    Jacob takes pride in being a Chinese born Canadian, specifically born in Vancouver. Admittidly he wishes he was a little bit more in tune with his asian roots. He also considers himself a big nerd in general, he likes games of all kinds and nerds out here and there about random facts like how a Volcano lead to the invention of bikes.
